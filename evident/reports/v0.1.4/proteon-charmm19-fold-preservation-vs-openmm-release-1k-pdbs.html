Claim

Across 1000 PDBs sampled (seed=42) from validation/pdbs/, proteon's CHARMM19+EEF1 minimization preserves fold within 0.005 TM-score of OpenMM CHARMM36+OBC2 minimization on the same inputs. Both runners use a 100-step L-BFGS-style minimizer at effective NoCutoff. The TM-score diff distribution is centred at median(openmm − proteon) ≈ +0.004, meaning OpenMM holds the fold marginally tighter on the typical structure but the implementations track each other to within experimentally-irrelevant fold perturbations across the corpus.

Replay command (verify-from-locked-JSONL today; full re-produce blocked on #42 + #48): python validation/tm_fold_preservation.py && python validation/tm_fold_preservation_openmm.py && python validation/fold_preservation/join_fold_preservation.py
